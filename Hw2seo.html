Understanding Search Engine Optimization-SEO


My understanding about SEO is to put your webpage in front of viewers. Kinda reminds me
of making a YouTube video. You do not want any viewers coming to your site, you want your
targeted audience or it's pointless.

SEO Checklist

  • Title Element
  • Keywords and Phrases
  • Description/Meta Tag
  • Content
  • Accessibility

Title Element

"It's important to use high-quality titles on your page"-Google

Sometimes it will be a struggle finding a title that will grab your targeted audience.

Keywords and Phrases

Keywords and phrases will help your site get picked up by search engines. Keywords will
bring traffic to your webpage, make sure you use keywords and phrases that will bring the
you are trying to attract. Example if you are making a site about Whale Watching, and add
phrases "Come see the sea turtles." Your target audience is Whale Watchers not people that
want to see sea turtles, that is pointless.

Start your title tag with a keyword the keyword should be at the top of the page. You
look for about 20-30 popular phrases that you believe your targeted audience will be
looking for. I know when I Google something I am looking for similar words before I
click on the link. Say if I do click on the link and it has nothing to do with
what I am looking for I leave the website.
