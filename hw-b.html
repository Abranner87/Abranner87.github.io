My Favorite Books

It's All In Your Head By Russ

One of my favorite books is It's All In Your Head by Russ. I can read this book
million times. I fall in love with this book every time I read or listen to it on
audio, I love this book so much I have the hard copy and the audio.

The chapter that speaks to me the most is "Don't Fall For It". Which starts on
page 113 as I was listening to this chapter the author said something that opened
my eyes "The reason someone will tell you something is unrealistic is that you
haven't done it yet, and deeper than that, THEY themselves haven't done it yet."

This chapter made me realize I do not need to play it safe and work the typical
"Good Career Job". I was doing what others from my community would've been
happy with what they would settle with. This chapter helped me decide to go back
to school and follow my dreams.
