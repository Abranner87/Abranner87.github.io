The Sunflower

Sunflower Facts

Sunflowers aren't just beautiful flowers they're very useful sunflowers
have used for healing, food, and oil for thousands of years.
The sunflower are native to the Native Americas,
as far back as 3000 BCE, when they were developed for food, medicine,dye
and oil.Then they exported to the rest if the world by Spanish
conquistadors around 1500.

Did You Know

Sunflower oil is a great source
of vitamin A and vitamin D, as well as Iron
and Calcium. Even it's no sunlight,there is still sunflower oil to provide
your daily dose of vitamin D! The sunflower looks like the sun, but they need
a lot of sun, a sunflower needs at least six to eight hours of direct sunlight every day.

Sunflowers have been used to create a substitute for peanut butter, known as sunbutter.
